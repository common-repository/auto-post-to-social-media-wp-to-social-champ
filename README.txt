It sends WordPress Pages, Posts or Custom Post Types to your Social Champ (SocialChamp.io) account for immediate or scheduled publishing to social networks.

== Description ==

Social Champ is one of the best ways to drive traffic, increase engagement and save time on social media. A result-driven social media management tool for scheduling posts, analyzing performance, and best content suggestions.

Whenever you schedule or update a post, page, or custom post, the Auto Post to Social Champ plugin will automatically share on your social media profile(s) or schedule that post to your socialchamp.io account.

If you don’t have a Social Champ account, [signup](https://www.socialchamp.io/signup "Social Champ") here!

### Smart Automation
Auto Post to Social Champ is a powerful plugin, which allows you to add your content to the Social Champ queue for smart automation or share the post on selected social profiles right away from the WordPress dashboard.

Using multiple tags, you can build a default format for the posts by pulling in the post titles, featured images, content, and more from the WordPress posts. With Social Champ, you would be able to track the analytics to grow and sustain your web traffic and the social media following.

### Easy Connection
You no longer have to watch monotonous tutorials to connect your social media using complicated APIs and integration. With this plugin, you can connect your Social Champ account to the plugin in a few easy steps.

Even connecting your social media accounts to Social Champ takes mere minutes.

Just choose the profiles you want to post content to from the plugin, set your default settings for posts and pages, and start scheduling!

### Features of Auto Post to Social Champ Plugin
* Auto-posting content from the WordPress posts, pages, and custom post types to social profiles when adding a new one or updating an old one.
* Define the exact parameters for pulling in content from WordPress posts.
* WordPress to Facebook pages and groups
* WordPress to Instagram Business
* WordPress to LinkedIn pages and groups
* WordPress to Pinterest boards
* WordPress to Twitter profiles
* WordPress to Google My Business listings

### Logs
In the logs section, you will get notifications of the following:
* Content that was successfully posted.
* Content that failed to post due to some reason.

== Installation ==

1. Login to your WordPress dashboard.
2. Navigate to Plugins -> Add New, and search for Social Champ.
3. Install and Activate.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Will it work without a Social Champ account?

No, you should have an active account on [Social Champ](https://www.socialchamp.io/signup).

= Will my WordPress post, page, or custom post type share immediately or will it be scheduled?

The plugin allows you to publish immediately or schedule it to publish using Queue. Make sure you have a queue set up on the Social Champ account.

= Which social media profile(s) Social Champ supports?

Currently, Social Champ allows you to auto-post on the below profile(s).
* Facebook pages and groups
* Instagram Business
* LinkedIn pages and groups
* Pinterest boards
* Twitter profiles
* Google My Business listings
